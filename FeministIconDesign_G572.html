Feminist Icon Design Workshop

Feminist cartographies are a transdisciplinary field and social movement that combines feminist theory and mapping practice. A feminist map can be defined as the (often) visual expression of spatial information that addresses questions of power, inequality, representation, and ethics. A map may be feminist in content, form, and/or process.

In this workshop, you will create your own feminist maps beginning with icon design.
