Feminist Icon Design Workshop

University of Kentucky



What is feminist mapping and where can we start? In this workshop, I’ll (briefly) review feminist mappings’ past, present, and futures before illustrating core feminist principles applied to spatial data, map design, and mapping workflows. Next, I’ll introduce Maki icons from Mapbox and we’ll apply the feminist principles as we iteratively redesign the icon set, one icon at a time.

All you need is scratch paper and something to sketch with! I’ll argue that feminist theory can generate alternative mapping vocabularies that work towards more equitable futures.
