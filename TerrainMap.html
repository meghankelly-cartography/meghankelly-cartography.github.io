Terrain Map

This is my first attempt at terrain representation. It was a project for an advanced cartographic design course at the University of Wisconsin–Madison.

While the bike route is imaginary, the terrain was created using a 10 million scale DEM of South America. ArcMap and Terrain Sculptor were used for raster processing, Photoshop for land cover and textures, and Illustrator to add vector data and type.
