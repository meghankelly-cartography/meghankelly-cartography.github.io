About Me

I'm a cartographer and graduate student at the University of Wisconsin–Madison in the department of Geography. Broadly speaking, I'm interested in all things mapping related. More specifically, I focus on maps and their relationship to visual storytelling. I'm greatly influenced by critical cartography and feminist theory as I'm always trying to rethink the ways in which we map and think about mapmaking and visual stories.

Stay tuned to this website for updates!
