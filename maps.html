Maps

My portfolio is a collection of various projects and assignments that I have completed in the last few years at the University of Kansas and the University of Wisconsin. I work across print and online mediums. My work focuses on maps and their relationship to visual storytelling. I'm greatly influenced by critical cartography and feminist theory as I'm always trying to rethink the ways in which we map and think about mapmaking and visual stories.
